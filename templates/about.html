About AlphaPolyp

Our Mission

AlphaPolyp is dedicated to improving polyp detection and measurement in endoscopy procedures through advanced artificial intelligence. Our goal is to provide healthcare professionals with accurate, real-time analysis tools that enhance clinical decision-making and improve patient outcomes.

The Technology

Our AI model uses a custom architecture combining Residual Atrous Pyramid Units (RAPU) with a CAFormer backbone to achieve state-of-the-art polyp segmentation and volume estimation. The model has been trained on a diverse dataset of endoscopy images to ensure accuracy across various clinical scenarios.

Key Features

  • Precise polyp segmentation with pixel-level accuracy
  • Volumetric estimation based on 3D modeling
  • Fast processing for real-time clinical use
  • User-friendly interface for healthcare professionals
